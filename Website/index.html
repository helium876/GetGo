GetGo

GetGo is the Easiest way to provide on-demand delivery service to users.

Getgo is a smartphone app which provides on-demand Crowdsourced delivery service to users. It connects available persons who are willing to deliver goods to others who need goods delivered.

Services

We are redefining how you use delivery services, it's not just for companies, Its for eveyone!. The platform will crowdsource your everyday deliveries and errands at an affordable cost

We are a team based at the University of Technology. Our mobile application is designed to take advantage of its users free time.
